Questions schools ask us

Do we need new computers or special internet?

No. Any computer or smartphone from the last decade with a browser works. Teachers mark attendance offline on their phones and it syncs when network returns. The headteacher's Pulse arrives by SMS — no internet needed at all.

How do parents pay fees?

Every invoice carries a control number. Parents pay via M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, NMB or CRDB — from anywhere. The receipt posts automatically and the bursar sees it instantly. Cash at the office is supported with dual-control confirmation.

Is our school's data safe?

Each school's data is isolated by the database engine itself, the audit trail is immutable (not even system administrators can rewrite it), and data stays in-region in line with the PDPA 2022. We'll happily walk your auditor through it.

What if we already use another system?

We import your students, guardians and fee balances from Excel in one onboarding session (about half a day), and run the first term alongside your current process so nothing is lost.

Does the AI replace teachers?

Never. The AI drafts — a teacher or headteacher reads, edits and approves every comment and every message before it reaches a parent or a report card. It saves hours; it doesn't take decisions.
